# SaaS-Market-Expansion-Business-Case

Excel-based strategic and financial model evaluating the expansion of a SaaS business into the North American market.

This project analyses market opportunity, investment requirements, revenue growth potential, and overall profitability to support a data-driven expansion decision.

The model simulates how a SaaS company operating in Europe could scale by entering a larger market and evaluates whether the expansion generates attractive financial returns.

---

# Overview

This project builds a simplified but structured business case for SaaS market expansion.

It combines market sizing, revenue modelling, cost estimation, growth projections and financial performance indicators.

The model answers three main strategic questions:

- How large is the potential market opportunity?
- How much investment is required to enter the new market?
- Does the expansion generate sustainable long-term profitability?

The analysis focuses on a **North American expansion scenario** for a digital productivity SaaS platform.

---

# How to Use the Model

# Project Structure

The project is composed of four interconnected Excel models.

## 1. Expansion Scenario

Defines the baseline assumptions for the expansion.

Includes:

- current market performance
- pricing assumptions
- potential user base
- market share estimates

This sheet establishes the revenue scenario used in the rest of the model.

---

## 2. Market Expansion Investment Analysis

Estimates the investment required to launch operations in the new market.

Includes:

- marketing campaign costs
- SaaS team setup
- infrastructure investment
- legal and compliance costs
- localization costs

It also provides a visual **investment breakdown** and calculates the expected payback period.

---

## 3. Revenue Growth Projection

Projects long-term revenue growth based on user growth and pricing assumptions.

Includes:

- initial user base
- annual growth rate
- monthly and annual revenue projections
- a 5-year revenue growth curve

This section illustrates the scalability potential of the SaaS business.

---

## 4. Investment Decision Analysis

Combines revenue and cost data to evaluate the overall financial attractiveness of the expansion.

Key outputs include:

- total expansion investment
- projected revenue
- net profit
- return on investment (ROI)
- payback period
- strategic recommendation

This sheet provides the final **go / no-go decision framework**.

---

# Key Financial Metrics

The model evaluates expansion performance using several financial indicators:

- **Revenue Growth** — projected scaling of the SaaS platform
- **Total Investment Required** — cost of entering the new market
- **Return on Investment (ROI)** — profitability of the expansion
- **Payback Period** — time required to recover the initial investment

Together these metrics help assess whether market expansion is financially justified.
